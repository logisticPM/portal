## Notes carried from the feasibility review
- **Most de-risked, partner-independent backbone:** StatCan IPEA ($63.7B GDI, 2023), federal 5% procurement open data (FY23-24: $1.24B / 6.11%), and the legal/policy corpus (DRIPA, UNDRIP action plans, Tsilhqot'in, Cowichan, TRC). All open, current, citable — **no partner provision needed.** This is what makes **E1** the lowest-data-risk direction.
- Hero sim (**E3**): credible at **2 sectors (housing + energy)**, not 4 — food and forest-carbon data are thin/gated.
- **E8** parked: federal/CCIB directories have no public API/bulk export; transactional tool risks reproducing what a relational economy critiques.
